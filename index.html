About Balc


BALC CADD Training Institute stands as one of the premier and largest institutions in Karnataka, holding an ISO 9001:2015 certification. Established in Bangalore, BALC CADD CENTRE has been a beacon of quality education since its inception in 2004, driven by the mission of “Computer Education for All.”

In an era marked by rapid advancements in science, medicine, and information technology, the world has indeed transformed into a global village. The pervasive use of information technology has underscored the critical importance of computer education, integrating it into the curricula of schools and colleges at almost every level. Despite this, traditional educational frameworks have often fallen short in imparting the necessary skills to individuals.

BALC has introduced many computer courses which can compete with any rapid and gradual changes in the IT field. The computer courses of BALC enable students to get adequate skills which can help in their academic and career.
